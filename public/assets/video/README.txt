Drop the M Tower 3D video here as `mtower-3d.mp4`.

Recommended encoding:
- H.264 (level 4.1 or below for broad compatibility)
- 1920x1080, 24-30 fps
- Bitrate around 4-6 Mbps
- AAC audio at 128 kbps OR completely stripped (the hero auto-plays muted)
- Loopable: the first frame should match the last to avoid jump cuts

If the file is missing or fails to load, the page falls back to the
static poster (public/assets/images/site/mtower-real.jpg).
